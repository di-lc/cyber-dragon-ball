Cyberstorm

Kael Takeda, known in the digital underworld as “Cyberstorm”, is a prodigy programmer with a razor-sharp mind and lightning-fast reflexes. A master of cybersecurity and artificial intelligence, he commands programming languages like they’re mere extensions of his own thoughts.

CodePixie

Lía Nakamura, known in the cyber circles as “CodePixie”, is a brilliant programmer with a natural talent for cracking patterns and mastering algorithms. With unmatched skills in AI development, she crafts cutting-edge systems and shields sensitive data from digital threats with surgical precision.

HexBlade

Ryo Tanaka, known in the shadows of cyberspace as “HexBlade”, is a skilled hacker who thrives in the dark corners of the digital world. A master of reverse engineering and cryptography, he blends brute force with razor-sharp intellect to break through even the most unbreakable firewalls.

NeonPulse

Ayla Vega, known across the stars as “NeonPulse”, is a space-born programming prodigy. Raised on an orbital station, she wields coding languages like a second tongue. A true specialist in artificial intelligence and cybersecurity, her skills pulse with the rhythm of the digital cosmos.

Codebreaker

In the heart of an abandoned space station, Orion Vex—a former cybernetic engineer—searches for a code lost for centuries. Rumor has it this code holds the key to an ancient technology powerful enough to redefine artificial intelligence itself.
